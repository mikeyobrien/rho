rho is a persistent AI operator for your own machines and accounts.

The Android app is a native wrapper around rho-web that lets you connect to your self-hosted rho server, manage sessions, and keep long-running responses alive with an explicit Live Mode foreground service.

Highlights:
- persistent memory and task management
- web workspace optimized for mobile control
- optional Live Mode foreground service for lock-screen continuity
- local-first setup with your own provider credentials
- no Firebase dependency required for baseline background reliability

This app is intended for people running rho themselves. You can connect to localhost during development, to LAN/Tailscale hosts you control, or to an HTTPS-accessible rho deployment.
